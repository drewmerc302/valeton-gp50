NAM and Preset Converter

Two independent tools: distill NAM captures for the pedal, and convert presets between the GP-5 and GP-50.

NAM Capture  A2 → A1

Distill a modern NAM A2 capture down to the A1 architecture the pedal can load. Renders a DI through the A2 model and trains an A1 model to reproduce it, then exports a device-ready .nam.

    2. Config

    How many passes the A1 model trains for. More = closer to the A2 tone, but slower (roughly linear).

    • 5–20 — quick preview. Rough, artifacts likely; use only to sanity-check a file.
    • 40–80 — the sweet spot. Faithful tone at a sane runtime. 80 is the default.
    • 100–200 — chasing the last few %. Diminishing returns and noticeably longer.
